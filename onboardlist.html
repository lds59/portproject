Problem

For OnboardList, the CEO wanted a redesign of the website before sharing it to potential investors.

Solution

OnboardList is an onboarding tool that is affordable for other makers, this was developed and designed as a way to onboard new users that is more effective than product tours.

Early Implementing

For early implementing, I referred to other Saas websites, like Slack, Spotify and Dropbox. From there I was able to come up with an inital redesign mockup for OnboardList.

Brand Development

Before diving into the second design iteration, I had to figure out what exactly OnboardList's brand was. In order to do this, I did a five word pairing exercise to determine brand words. I started off by listing what I wanted, followed by what I don't that is somewhat related to what I want but may be a nagative for the brand. Next I came up with the word that actually represents the brand using this equation.

Want - Don't Want = Brand Word.

These are the words I came up with to determind the brand.
